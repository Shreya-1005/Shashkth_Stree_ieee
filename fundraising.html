Welcome to Shashkth Stree

We are here to help everyone in need

We are dedicated to empowering women and children, giving them the tools, support, and opportunities to rebuild their lives and create brighter futures. Our mission is to provide safe spaces, access to education, skill-building programs, and essential resources to help women regain their independence and confidence while ensuring children receive the care, nourishment, and guidance they need to thrive. By addressing their immediate needs and fostering long-term growth, we aim to break cycles of hardship and inspire lasting change. Together, we can transform lives and build a world where every woman and child has the chance to rise and succeed.
